Dark Cloud Cover Pattern Info
Dark Cloud Cover
Green candle followed by red candle with gap up and penetration
Pattern Characteristics:
  • First candle: Bullish with substantial body
  • Second candle: Bearish with gap up
  • Closes below midpoint of first candle
  • Shows shift from bullish to bearish sentiment
Key Insights:
  • Strong bearish reversal signal
  • Works best at end of uptrends
  • Deeper penetration = stronger signal
  • Volume confirmation helps
