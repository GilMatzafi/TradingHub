Bearish Kicker
Gap down with no overlap showing bearish momentum
Pattern Characteristics:
  • Bullish: Gap up after downtrend
  • Bearish: Gap down after uptrend
  • No overlap between candle bodies
  • Sudden and decisive sentiment change
Key Insights:
  • One of the most powerful reversal patterns
  • Signals complete sentiment reversal
  • Often marks beginning of strong trends
  • Reflects sudden shift in psychology
