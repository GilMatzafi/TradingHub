Piercing Line Pattern Info
Piercing Line (Bullish)
Red candle followed by green candle that opens below the red candle's low but closes above its midpoint
Key Insights:
  • Strong bullish reversal signal
  • Works best at support levels
  • Second candle must pierce at least 50% of first candle's body
  • Trend context important
  • Volume confirmation helps
Pattern Conditions:
  • First candle: Bearish (red) with significant body
  • Second candle: Opens below first candle's low
  • Second candle: Closes above midpoint of first candle's body
  • Second candle: Bullish (green)
