Morning Star Pattern Info
Morning Star
Long bearish → Gap down star → Strong bullish confirmation
Pattern Characteristics:
  • First: Long bearish candle
  • Second: Small star with gap down
  • Third: Strong bullish confirmation
  • Third closes above first candle's midpoint
Key Insights:
  • One of the most reliable reversal patterns
  • Gap down shows continued pessimism
  • Star candle indicates indecision
  • Third candle shows buyers taking control
