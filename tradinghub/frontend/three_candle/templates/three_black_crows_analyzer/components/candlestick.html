Three Black Crows
Three consecutive bearish candles with progressive closes
Pattern Characteristics:
  • Three consecutive bearish candles
  • Each opens within previous candle's body
  • Progressive closes show sustained momentum
  • Short lower shadows indicate strong selling
Key Insights:
  • Strong bearish reversal signal
  • Shows sustained selling pressure
  • Gradual takeover by bears
  • Often marks beginning of downtrend
