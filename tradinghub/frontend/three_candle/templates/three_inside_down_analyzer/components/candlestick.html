Three Inside Down Pattern Info
Three Inside Down
Long bullish → Small bearish (Harami) → Strong bearish confirmation
Pattern Characteristics:
  • First: Long bullish candle
  • Second: Small bearish (Harami)
  • Third: Strong bearish confirmation
  • Third closes below first candle's low
Key Insights:
  • Strong bearish reversal signal
  • Harami shows weakening bullish momentum
  • Confirmation shows sellers taking control
  • Often marks beginning of downtrend
