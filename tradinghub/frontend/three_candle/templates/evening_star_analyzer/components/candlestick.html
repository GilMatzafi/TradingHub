Evening Star Pattern Info
Evening Star
Long bullish → Gap up star → Strong bearish confirmation
Pattern Characteristics:
  • First: Long bullish candle
  • Second: Small star with gap up
  • Third: Strong bearish confirmation
  • Third closes below first candle's midpoint
Key Insights:
  • One of the most reliable reversal patterns
  • Gap up shows continued optimism
  • Star candle indicates indecision
  • Third candle shows sellers taking control
