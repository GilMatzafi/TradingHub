Three White Soldiers Pattern Info
Three White Soldiers
Three consecutive bullish candles with progressive closes
Pattern Characteristics:
  • Three consecutive bullish candles
  • Each opens within previous candle's body
  • Progressive closes show sustained momentum
  • Short upper shadows indicate strong buying
Key Insights:
  • Strong bullish reversal signal
  • Shows sustained buying pressure
  • Gradual takeover by bulls
  • Often marks beginning of uptrend
